Massage Services

Jones Strain Counterstrain

Jones Strain Counterstrain uses a non-invasive positional release of the muscular and nervous system. This has been found to alleviate both chronic and dysfunctional pain. Many passive positional stretches are used.
Wikipedia - Counterstrain

Treatment Massage

Treatment massage is focused on specific areas of the body and achieving certain goals such as reducing and eliminating chronic pain areas. Recovery from an acute injury, pre or post surgery. Sometimes treatment massage can be tender or slightly painful during the massage itself , though, after the massage the benefits of the work can be felt. Pressure can range from light to very firm. Often dependent on the tender points felt by the client.

Swedish Massage

Swedish massage uses a combination of various movement including linear and cross fiber frictions, effleurage and kneading this traditional method of massage is very helpful in the loosening of tight muscles, soothes sore muscles and increased overall circulation. Relaxation massage can also help reduce stress related illness. Pressure can range from very light to moderate.

Pregnancy Massage

Pregnancy massage helps reduce stress, blood pressure, swelling, and improve circulation to weight bearing joints such as hips, back and ankles. This massage is done with the client lying on their side with pillows and support cushions for their comfort.
